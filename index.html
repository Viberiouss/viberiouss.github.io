Welcome to RetroWare


This program has very single game from every* major 3D console (including some major 2d consoles) up to the Xbox 360 and the PS3. There are also bundled some early Nintendo Switch games.



How to change the directory of installed games:


Go to your Chrome Download Settings and then change the directory of your desired file to the micro sd, hard drive, or folder.



How to extract .zip, .7z, or .rar:

Go to 7-zip.org. Whenever you download 7zip, the app will automatically detect when you try to extract a zipped file, open the app, and have a giant button to extract the files will appear.
